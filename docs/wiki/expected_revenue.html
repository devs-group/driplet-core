\mathit{\#~of~users} \times \mathit{avg~value~of~user}

\mathit{\#users} \times \mathit{\#audiences~per~user} \times \mathit{\#customers} \times \mathit{price~per~audience}

500\,\text{k} \times 3 \times 100 \times \$5 = \$750\,\text{M}
